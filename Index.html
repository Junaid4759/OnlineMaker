FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common things clients ask us.

How long will it take to build my website?

A basic landing page can be delivered in as little as 3-5 business days. An e-commerce site or a more complex business website typically takes 1-2 weeks. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of the project and how quickly you provide us with the necessary content and feedback.

What do I need to provide?

You will need to provide the text content (e.g., about your business, service descriptions), any images you want to use (like logos or product photos), and your branding guidelines (colors, fonts). If you don't have content, we can help you with placeholder text and stock images to get started.

Is hosting and domain included in the price?

Our packages cover the website design and development cost. Domain name registration and web hosting are separate, recurring costs. However, we provide full assistance in choosing the best, most affordable hosting and domain providers and can set everything up for you.

Can I update the website myself later?

Absolutely! If your website is built on a Content Management System (CMS) like WordPress, we provide a basic training session to show you how to update text, images, and blog posts. For websites built with raw code, updates would typically require our assistance.
